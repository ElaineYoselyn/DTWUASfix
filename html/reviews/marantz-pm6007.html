Marantz PM6007

The Marantz PM6007 is a stereo integrated amplifier that blends classic design with modern features. Known for its musicality and high-quality audio reproduction, this amplifier is a perfect centerpiece for a two-channel audio system. With its sleek aesthetics and impressive performance, the PM6007 caters to audiophiles seeking a refined listening experience.

Sound: The PM6007 upholds Marantz's reputation for delivering a warm and engaging sound. With its high-current discrete power amplification and carefully tuned components, it reproduces music with clarity and precision. The amplifier's musicality shines across various genres, making it a favorite among enthusiasts appreciative of rich, detailed audio.
Design: Marantz maintains its classic design language with the PM6007, featuring a clean front panel, large control knobs, and a sturdy chassis. The attention to detail in construction reflects the brand's commitment to both form and function. While the design is more traditional, it exudes a timeless elegance.
Price: Positioned in the mid-range market, the PM6007 offers a balance between performance and affordability. The amplifier provides a solid entry point for those seeking high-quality audio without venturing into the premium price range, making it accessible to a broader audience.
Performance: The PM6007 incorporates modern features, including digital inputs and a built-in DAC (Digital-to-Analog Converter), catering to users with both analog and digital sources. The inclusion of a phono stage for vinyl enthusiasts adds versatility, enhancing its appeal to a diverse audience.
